OUR STRATEGY

The Demand

Now more than ever, people are enticed by effortless pickup and delivery options. As food delivery services prove to be remarkably successful with a surprisingly large amount of interest, there is most certainly a market for the same service in other industries.

The Satisfaction

Orders will be delivered within a range of 1 to 4 hours. The customer will have a 24-hour window to decide if he or she is satisfied with their purchase. If not, they may choose to return through an Instant Apparel driver, free of charge.

The Local

With only a few clicks, Instant Apparel’s mobile app, you can quickly and effectively look up all of the clothing stores near you that are open and available for delivery any orders.

The Speed

The fast delivery of Instant Apparel will get your clothing orders depends on a number of things. How far is the store? How bad is traffic? The average time is approximately 60 minutes but it could be more or less. Either way, you can track where your driver is on the Instant Apparel website or app.
